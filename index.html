Member of Hack Club!


I found out about Hack Club in late November 2024 and have been a proud member of the community since!
My first project was coding a game from my Sprig, which is a custom console that Hack Club has to offer to teens who are up to the challenge of coding a game to run on it! Hack Club has given me a reason to code, before I made small projects occasionally but always lost a reason to keep coding. With the encouragement of this fabulous community of hackers and creators, I have come farther than I ever could have imagined! Most code I have written today wouldn't exist were it not for hack club. I am a proud member, and encourage all who are below 18 and interested in joining to hop onboard; it has changed my life!
